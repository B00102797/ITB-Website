The History Of ITB

ITB opened in 1999 and the first building was A Block.

The cement wasn't even properly dried when the first people entered, and had to walk around it.

The first ITB Logo was a disaster.

The Red and Green colouration meant it was very difficult for people with visual difficulties to see.

Advertisements for ITB would run on the radio.

The plot of land ITB is currently on was meant to be for the shopping centre

The first ceremony was held in 2001, just two years after the college had opened.

The idea for TUD started in 2012 and the legislation was just passed this year, 2018.

CDM, my course, Creative Digital Media, was launched in 2009.
